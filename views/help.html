Help Information about this site!

What is NUShare?

“NUShare” is an online portal designed for the students at Northeastern University to connect with other students on campus and help to ease their study-life. It is a customized form of social media with features that enable students to have easy access to resources in a quick, organized and efficient manner. NUShare has two main functionalities, Housing Reviews and Book-Share.

Who is NUShare for?

NUShare is basically designed for students studying at Northeastern University in order to have access to resources currently not provided by the university website.

How is BookShare helpful for our users?

Book-Share is a resource that the user can use to borrow textbooks from other students studying in Northeastern, in case it is unavailable in the library or too expensive to be brought online. A student can also post a book for lending.

How useful are the Housing Reviews mentioned on NUShare to our users?

Housing Reviews page consists honest reviews about various apartments and neighbourhoods near Northeastern University that the students can refer while renting out apartments. A student can also write a review and help out fellow students in their house hunt.
